How I got started

My dad showed me the game of golf at age five and I was hooked ever since. It's a great way to unwind for me and provides an amazing challenge.

I grew up playing the public courses in Spokane, WA and the Eagle Ridge Par 3 course near our residence. When my family moved to Sammamish, WA and joined the Plateau Club, I was able to play the game frequently,took several lessons and made friends while playing the course.

My skill level has varied depending on my stage in life. Right now, am a 20.2 handicap. I hope to be under an 18.0 handicap by the end of year.
